Explore Our Features

Career Pathfinder goes beyond advice — actionable proof, interactive insights, and gamified learning to help students explore, grow, and showcase their skills.

Micro-Project Generator

AI generates tiny, doable projects to instantly apply skills. Example: “Redesign a chatbot FAQ using Dialogflow.” Each project becomes proof of growth. These micro-projects are tailored to the student's current skill level, helping them build confidence and gain practical experience step by step. They are also shareable for resumes and LinkedIn portfolios.

Blockchain Skills Passport

Every completed project is a verifiable badge stored on-chain. Students carry proof anywhere — LinkedIn, college applications, internships. This creates a permanent and tamper-proof record of achievements. The blockchain passport ensures credibility and gives students a sense of accomplishment, motivating them to consistently improve and showcase their skills.

Culture Fit Layer

Lifestyle match insights: client interactions, remote work hours, creativity vs routine. Example: “This role requires client meetings across time zones. Enjoy high communication roles?” This helps students align their career choices with personal preferences, working style, and life goals, reducing the risk of burnout and enhancing long-term satisfaction.

Career Paths

Students can explore curated career paths tailored to their interests, skills, and aspirations. Each path includes recommended courses, internships, and micro-projects. Visual roadmaps allow students to see milestones, prerequisites, and the journey toward their dream career, helping them make informed decisions and track progress.

Skills Map

interactive chart that highlights current skills in green and missing skills in red. Students can easily see gaps and set priorities to bridge them. The Skills Map provides a clear visual representation of their growth trajectory, making learning structured and measurable while fostering motivation to complete skill sets for career readiness.

Projects & Proof

A consolidated list of micro-projects with badges earned, providing tangible evidence of skill development. Students can click on projects to view details, submission status, and outcomes. This section encourages continuous hands-on practice and helps build a credible portfolio that stands out to recruiters and mentors.

Simulation

Immersive “day in the life” simulations of different careers, showing real tasks, challenges, schedules, and environments. Students can virtually experience roles like AI Product Manager, Data Analyst, or Engineer before committing. This helps them make more confident career choices and understand what to expect in their future workplace.
